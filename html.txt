                <h3>Raigad Fort</h3>
                <p>Raigad is a historically significant district in the Konkan region of Maharashtra, known for its rich cultural heritage and natural beauty. The most iconic attraction is Raigad Fort, which was the capital of the Maratha Empire under Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj. Perched atop a hill at an elevation of around 2,700 feet, the fort offers stunning views of the surrounding valleys and hills, and is accessible by a steep climb or via a ropeway.</p>
